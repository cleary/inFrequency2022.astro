About

inFREQUENCY is a pair of Workshops and Performances by cutting edge electronic Artists from the South Coast and Western NSW.
Tickets are now available for the Western Riverina Worshops, running in Yanco, NSW (near Leeton/Griffith)

The inFREQUENCY tour is supported by the Backroads Initiative and South East Arts through funding from Create NSW and the NSW Government.
